Our Journey

Global Presence

Swanson Plastics Corporation was established in 1986. To expand internationalization, we invested in China, Singapore, Malaysia, India, and Indonesia starting from 1992. We work with renowned international companies to achieve sustainable development.

Growth in India

Established in 2010 in Goa, Swanson Plastics India evolved from one machine to three advanced production lines. This reflects our commitment to quality and the high-performance plastic solutions demand in India.
