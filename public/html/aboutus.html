About Us

USI Corporation invested in the establishment of Swanson Plastics Corporation in 1986 to expand services with the production and sales of polyethylene (PE) products.

Since its establishment, Swanson Plastics Corporation has upheld a stable and steadfast business philosophy and continuously improved overall operation efficiency and service quality for the processing, production, and development of polyethylene film. To expand the scope of business operations and accelerate internationalization, the Company began investments in Mainland China, Singapore, Malaysia, India, and Indonesia starting from 1992. We work with Renowned international companies in technical cooperation and R&D to achieve sustainable development for businesses and continuous growth. Swanson currently has 7 plants in Taiwan and overseas to meet local demands and market products in main overseas markets. The Company continues to advance its expansion plan. In addition to enhancing investments in other regions, we actively invest in the expansion of related product lines with the aim of providing customers with more satisfying products and more comprehensive services.

Swanson Plastics India – A Journey of Growth and Innovation

Established in 2010, Swanson Plastics India laid its foundation in Bhuipal Honda, Goa, with a passionate and energetic team. What began as a modest operation with a single machine has evolved into a steadily expanding facility, now operating three advanced machines. This steady growth reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and the growing demand for high-performance plastic solutions in India.

Driven by a bullish outlook on the Indian market, our expansion plans are well underway, backed by the group's deep belief in the country’s economic potential and vibrant talent pool. India’s growth trajectory continues to inspire confidence, and at Swanson Plastics, we strongly believe that the youth—India’s greatest asset—will play a pivotal role in shaping the future of the manufacturing industry.

Our journey is marked not only by technological advancement but also by our focus on nurturing local talent, fostering innovation, and maintaining the highest standards of sustainability and quality. As we move forward, Swanson Plastics India remains dedicated to being a key contributor to India's industrial progress and a trusted partner in the global plastics industry.
